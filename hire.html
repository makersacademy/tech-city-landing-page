Who are our Hiring Partners?

We work with everyone from high-growth start-ups to established corporates seeking to update their tech teams and high-profile agencies looking for best practice evangelists.

What unites our Fellowship partners is a commitment to new talent, to meritocracy and to growing their own. We support you on selection, training and mentoring through the internship and into a full-time job.

Hiring through the Tech City Fellowship reduces your recruitment fees and diversifies your team.
